Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete each bundle?
The A0-A2 Foundation bundle typically takes 3-4 months with 6 hours/week of study, while the A0-B2 Mastery bundle takes 6-8 months. However, you have lifetime access to all materials and can learn at your own pace.
What support is included with each bundle?
Both bundles include comprehensive support features. The Foundation bundle offers weekly live sessions and tutor support, while the Mastery bundle provides 1-on-1 mentorship and personalized live sessions for enhanced learning.
Are the bundles suitable for complete beginners?
Yes! Both bundles start from A0 (complete beginner) level and progressively build your skills. Our structured approach ensures you have a strong foundation before advancing to more complex topics.
What happens after I purchase a bundle?
Upon purchase, you'll receive immediate access to all course materials, study resources, and practice quizzes. You'll also be able to schedule your first live session or mentorship meeting to begin your learning journey.
What's the difference between the Foundation and Mastery bundles?
The Foundation bundle (A0-A2) focuses on building elementary French skills, perfect for basic communication and achieving CLB 5. The Mastery bundle (A0-B2) is more comprehensive, taking you to an upper-intermediate level with CLB 7, including advanced topics and personalized mentorship.
Can I access the course materials on mobile devices?
Yes! All course materials are fully responsive and can be accessed on any device - smartphones, tablets, or computers. You can learn on-the-go and sync your progress across all your devices.
What study materials are included?
Both bundles include comprehensive study materials: downloadable eBooks, grammar guides, vocabulary lists, audio lessons, video tutorials, practice exercises, mock tests, and cultural insights. The Mastery bundle includes additional advanced materials and specialized content.
