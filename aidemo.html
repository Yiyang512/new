    Example Correction

    ⟲ Original Text

    Je veux un café avec du lait. Je suis ici depuis deux jour.

    Common mistakes:
    • Using "veux" (informal)
    • Missing 's' in "jours" (plural)

    ✓ Corrected Version

    Je voudrais un café avec du lait. Je suis ici depuis deux jours.

    Improvements:
    • "voudrais" (more polite)
    • Correct plural form "jours"

    Detailed Explanation:

    1. Politeness Level: "Je voudrais" is the conditional form of "vouloir" and is more polite than "Je veux".
      Pronunciation: "zhuh voo-dreh" vs "zhuh vuh"
    2. Plural Agreement: Time expressions require plural form when quantity is more than one.
      Note: The 's' in "jours" is silent but required in writing
    ✓ Excellent! Your grammar and pronunciation are perfect!
    Strong points:
    • Correct verb conjugation
    • Proper word order
    • Polite form usage

    Common Correction Types

    Gender Agreement
    le/la, un/une
    Verb Conjugation
    -er, -ir, -re endings
    Plural Forms
    Adding -s, -x

    Writing Tips:

    • Start Formal: Always begin with formal French until you're sure of the context
    • Double-Check Plurals: Pay attention to quantity words that require plural forms
    • Mind Your Pronouns: Ensure proper agreement with subject pronouns
