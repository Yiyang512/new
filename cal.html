Understanding Your Results

French Language Points

Points are awarded based on your French language proficiency levels in all four abilities (reading, writing, listening, and speaking). Higher levels in French can significantly boost your CRS score.

Bilingual Bonus

You can earn up to 50 additional points if you demonstrate proficiency in both English and French. This bonus is available if you achieve CLB 7 or higher in French and CLB 5 or higher in English.

Transferability Points

Additional points may be available for combinations of language skills with education and work experience. These points can further increase your CRS score.
