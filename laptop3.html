Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Simple French with Hems different from traditional language schools?

Simple French with Hems is specifically engineered for immigration candidates with time-sensitive goals. We focus exclusively on the language skills assessed for immigration processes rather than general fluency. Our curriculum is aligned with TEF/TEFAQ and DELF examination standards, and we integrate immigration expertise directly into the language acquisition process.

Do I need any prior French knowledge to enroll?

No prior knowledge is required. Our program accommodates complete beginners through our Accelerated Foundation module. However, those with some basic French knowledge will progress more quickly through the initial phases. Our assessment identifies your precise starting point to ensure efficient progression.

How quickly can I expect to achieve CLB 7 for Express Entry?

Most candidates starting from beginner (A1) level achieve CLB 7 (B2) within 6-8 months with dedicated study using our intensive program. Those beginning at an intermediate level (A2-B1) typically reach CLB 7 in 3-4 months. Our guarantee ensures you'll have the necessary preparation to achieve this level within your immigration timeline.

Do you provide official TEF/TEFAQ testing?

While we're not an official test center, we have partnerships with certified TEF/TEFAQ testing centers across Canada and internationally. Our program includes guided registration for these exams, scheduling optimization, and transportation to testing centers where available. Our preparation is precisely aligned with the latest exam formats.
