How long does it take to get approved?
We review all applications within 48 hours. Once approved, you'll receive immediate access to your affiliate dashboard and professional promotional materials.
What is the cookie duration?
We offer a generous 90-day cookie duration, which means you'll earn a commission if your referral purchases within 90 days of clicking your affiliate link – one of the longest tracking periods in the industry.
When and how do I get paid?
Payments are processed reliably on the 15th of each month for the previous month's sales. We offer multiple secure payment options including PayPal, Wise, and direct bank transfers.
Is there a minimum payout threshold?
Yes, the minimum payout is $50 for Standard and Silver tier affiliates. Gold tier affiliates enjoy the privilege of no minimum payout requirement.
Can I promote the affiliate program to others?
We don't currently offer a two-tier affiliate system. Your commissions are earned only on direct sales you refer. We focus on maximizing the value of your direct referrals.
What promotional materials do you provide?
We provide comprehensive marketing resources including high-converting banners in various sizes, professional email templates, engaging social media content, blog post templates, and exclusive discounts for your audience.
Do you allow paid advertising with my affiliate link?
Yes, but with some strategic restrictions. You cannot bid on our brand terms in search engines to avoid competing with our own campaigns. Please contact your dedicated affiliate manager for complete guidelines on paid advertising opportunities.
Are there any restrictions on how I can promote?
We prohibit spam, misleading claims, and any unethical marketing tactics to maintain the integrity of our brand. Full guidelines are provided upon approval to ensure successful and compliant promotion.
